  1. Sliding Window Maximum

  • Solution without Deque

    • find the max of chunk of k from the left; each left[i] is the max from the start of the chunk (i % k == 0) to the end of the chunk at i.==> the max of the chunk is at the end of the chunk => left(i + k - 1)
    • find the max of chunk of k from the right (n - 1); each right[i] is the max from the end of the chun ((i + 1) % k == 0) to the start of the chunk. ==> the max of chunk is at the begining of the chunk. => right(i)
    • res[i] = Math.max(right[i], left[i + k - 1]);

  • Solution with Depque

    • use the deque to store the index
    • sliding the window by: remove the index if it’s out of the range of i - k + 1 (end of queue)
    • always keep the largest index at the front of the queue
    • store the max in the res[] when i >= k - 1
